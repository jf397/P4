Who am I?

My name is Jens Feldthus, I'm 23 years old and was born and raised in Copenhagen Denmark. I started playing volleyball at 9 years old and have since then played matches for the danish youth national teams, played two seasons in the best Danish league and four seasons at the Division 1 level in the NCAA. I'm a student athlete at NJIT competing at the D1 level as the captain of the men's volleybal team. I will be graduating in May 2023 and will be looking to play professional volleyball afterwards.
